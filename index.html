International Poverty Eradication Projects and Strategies (IPEPAS) analyse, prioritise, plan and raise funds for projects specifically aimed at ending poverty forever. It is unique in that its overall strategy is to only exist until its job is done. Each strategy, program, project, task and subtask is aimed at ending poverty withing a specified time.
