Projects 4 Self Reliance (P4SR) analyse, prioritise, plan and raise funds for projects specifically aimed at ending poverty forever. It is unique in that its overall strategy is to only exist until its job is done, instead of perpetuating handouts. Each strategy, program, project, task and subtask is aimed at ending poverty within a specified time.

 

MSCW PRIORITISATION

MUST HAVES
1. Nutritition: Community Feeding Scheme

27 month feeding scheme funded by P4SR with a stipend for those who work to feed the indigent. At the end of the 9 months, each participant will be a shareholder in a local market after 27 months will own a share in the new local mall. Malls create local jobs throughout its supply chain. This permanently frees participants from poverty.

2. Community Watch - Stipend
Community Watch in conjunction with community policing forums - set up with stipend for all patrollers and control room staff

3. Womens business centres
Every woman wishing to become independent will be able to work at the centre. Ultimately successful business women are created, and funding will be taken over by alumni.

4. Borehole Drilling - Divining
Provision of essential water supply to sustain residents in rural and informal settlements.
