Projects 4 Self Reliance (P4SR) analyse, prioritise, plan and raise funds for projects specifically aimed at ending poverty forever.

It is unique in that its overall strategy is to only exist until its job is done, instead of perpetuating handouts. Each strategy, program, project, task and subtask is aimed at ending poverty within a specified time.

MUST HAVES

1. Creating new employment stimulating Markets and Malls through community investment. We free community members by providing Nutritition through an innovative Community Feeding Scheme based at a distriburion centre which evolves into a local market which will be owned by the community at the end of 9 months.

27 month feeding scheme funded by P4SR with a stipend for those who work to feed the indigent. At the end of the first 9 months, each participant will be a shareholder in a local market. After 27 months participants will own a share in the new regional mall in conjunction with several other communities. Malls and markets create local jobs throughout their vast supply chains. This permanently frees participants from poverty.

2. Community Watch with stipend for patrollers.
Community Watch in conjunction with community policing forums - set up with stipend for all patrollers and control room staff. The first 9 months will be paid for by the fund, then community participants will take over stipends.

Every woman wishing to become independent will be able to work at the centre. Ultimately successful business women are created, and funding will be taken over by alumni.
